# Product Reference

## Product

Reddit Lead Generation SaaS

AI-powered social listening platform that detects high-intent leads on Reddit and helps users act on them quickly.

## Core Problem

Potential buyers often ask for recommendations, tools, services, or solutions on Reddit.

Those posts are high-signal, but they are easy to miss and become less valuable as they get older.

The product helps users detect these opportunities early, qualify them, and review them in one place.

## Product Value

Users can:

- monitor Reddit discussions relevant to their offer
- detect high-intent posts and comments
- receive alerts when new leads appear
- review AI-generated lead summaries
- use AI-generated reply suggestions manually
- manage leads in a dashboard workflow

## Example Lead

- Subreddit: `r/startups`
- Post: `"Looking for a CRM for my SaaS startup"`
- Intent: `HIGH`
- Posted: `12 minutes ago`

## MVP Scope

The MVP focuses on discovery first.

Included:

- authentication
- campaign creation
- subreddit suggestions
- Reddit lead detection
- lead feed/dashboard
- lead detail with AI analysis
- email alerts
- Slack alerts
- AI reply suggestions for manual use

Not included in the first version:

- automated Reddit posting
- automated commenting
- CRM integrations
- cross-platform social lead detection

## User Workflow

1. Sign in
2. Create a campaign
3. Define keywords, negative keywords, and subreddits
4. Wait for Reddit monitoring and lead scoring
5. Review leads in the dashboard
6. Save, ignore, or act on strong leads
